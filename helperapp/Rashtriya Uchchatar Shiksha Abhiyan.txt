raashtreey uchchatar shiksha abhiyaan ek kendr praayojit yojana hai, jisaka uddeshy 2013 mein raajy ke uchch shikshan sansthaanon ko rananeetik vitt poshan pradaan karana hai. kendreey vittaposhan maanak aadhaarit aur parinaam nirbhar hoga. maanyata praapt sansthaanon tak pahunchane se pahale raajy sarakaaron / kendr shaasit pradeshon ke maadhyam se kendreey mantraalay se raajy uchch shiksha parishadon ke lie dhan pravaah hoga. raajyon ko vitt poshan raajy uchch shiksha yojanaon ke mahatvapoorn moolyaankan ke aadhaar par kiya jaega, jo uchch shiksha mein ikvitee, pahunch aur utkrshtata ke muddon ko sambodhit karane ke lie pratyek raajy kee rananeeti ka varnan karega.
