2004 mein shuroo kee gaee, Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya yojana ka uddeshy uchch praathamik star par alpasankhyak samudaayon kee ladakiyon ke lie aavaaseey vidyaalay sthaapit karana hai. yah yojana mukhy roop se desh ke un hisson mein laagoo kee jaatee hai jahaan ladakiyon ko skool mein daakhila nahin diya jaata hai. yah yojana gareebee rekha se neeche vaale parivaaron kee ladakiyon ko 25% aur ST, SC, OBC, aur any alpasankhyak samudaayon kee ladakiyon ko 75% aarakshan pradaan karatee hai. is yojana ke peechhe mukhy vichaar yah hai ki aavaaseey vidyaalayon kee sthaapana karake, samaaj ke vanchit samoohon kee ladakiyaan gunavattaapoorn shiksha praapt kar sakatee hain.
