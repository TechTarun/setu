pradhaanamantree kaushal vikaas yojana raashtreey kaushal vikaas nigam dvaara kaaryaanvit kaushal vikaas aur udyamita mantraalay kee pramukh yojana hai. is kaushal pramaanan yojana ka uddeshy badee sankhya mein bhaarateey yuvaon ko udyog-sambandhit kaushal prashikshan lene mein saksham banaana hai jo unhen behatar aajeevika haasil karane mein madad karega. seekhane ke poorv anubhav ya kaushal vaale vyaktiyon ko bhee poorv shikshan kee maanyata ke tahat moolyaankan aur pramaanit kiya jaega.
