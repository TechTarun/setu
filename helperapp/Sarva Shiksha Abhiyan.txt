yah kaaryakram 2001 mein pesh kiya gaya tha aur yah bhaarat kee sabase badee pariyojanaon mein se ek hai. sarv shiksha abhiyaan bachchon ko yoonivarsal elimentree ejukeshan dilaane ke lie ek pramukh kaaryakram hai. yah kaaryakram poore desh ko kavar karata hai aur sthaaneey aur raajy sarakaaron ke saath saajhedaaree mein kaam karata hai. yah abhiyaan mukhy roop se 6 se 14 varsh kee aayu ke bachchon ke lie upayogee hai. kaaryakram ka uddeshy shiksha ko saarvabhaumik banaana hai aur samayabaddh kaaryaanvayan rananeeti aur sandarbh-vishisht yojana dvaara isakee gunavatta mein sudhaar karana hai. isamen sabhee saamaajik vargon ke bachche shaamil hain.
